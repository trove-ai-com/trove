Introducing DataIQ by Trove-AI:
Revolutionizing Content Interaction

We are thrilled to announce the launch of DataIQ, the latest innovation from Trove-AI. DataIQ is a groundbreaking Kubernetes-native solution designed to transform how you interact with your documents and images. Utilizing advanced OCR capabilities, DataIQ seamlessly processes text in over 100 languages, including PDFs, DOCs, and images, making them fully searchable and interactive.

DataIQ empowers users to ask questions and extract meaningful insights from their content like never before. With support for querying in over 150 native languages, you can find your answers in the language you are most comfortable with. Whether deployed in the cloud or on-premise, DataIQ offers unparalleled flexibility and scalability to meet your needs.

DataIQ can handle documents that are thousands of years old, unlocking the potential to explore and interact with historical texts in ways previously unimaginable.

Experience the future of content interaction with DataIQ by Trove-AI, where the power of AI meets the accessibility of everyday documents.
